Our Story

We Started Rackteon
to Make a Difference

At Rackteon Networks, we are a team of experienced IT engineers who started this journey in Kashmir, India — where we saw talented young people struggle to find quality, affordable IT training. We decided to change that. Today, our bootcamp is open to students across India and worldwide, giving anyone with a laptop and an internet connection the skills they need to build a real career in networking.

🎯

Our Mission

Provide high-quality, practical, and affordable training so no deserving student is left behind due to financial limitations.

🌏

Our Vision

A future where students across India and around the world are equipped with real-world IT skills, confident, and ready to step into the global job market.

💡

Our Approach

Expert instruction, hands-on labs, career-focused training, and free mock interviews — all at a price that doesn't break the bank.

Why Just ₹6,800?

We believe great education should not be a privilege. The IT industry is full of opportunity — from India to the rest of the world — and yet quality training is often priced out of reach for most students. Rackteon Networks was built to fix that. Our complete networking bootcamp is offered at just ₹6,800, which is less than most basic CCNA courses, with zero compromise on content, quality, or career support. Every student who works hard deserves a real shot at a great IT career.
